Privacy Policy

Summary

SafeSpaces is a text-only safety tool that detects risky patterns and alerts parents. We do not collect images, audio, video, or location data.

Data We Collect

  • Safety alerts (category, timestamp, and redacted text snippet)
  • Child identifier you set in the extension (for grouping alerts)
  • Page origin + path (no query parameters)

How We Use Data

  • To display alerts and safety summaries in the parent dashboard
  • To improve rules and reduce false positives (aggregated)

What We Do Not Collect

  • Full message content or full URLs (query strings are removed)
  • Images, audio, video, or location data
  • Contacts or social graphs

Data Retention

Alerts are retained to provide history in the dashboard. You can request deletion by contacting us.
